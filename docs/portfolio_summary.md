## Portfolio Projects

### California Seasonal Temperature Analysis

Evaluates seasonal temperature patterns and anomalies across California using long-term climate observations to identify regional temperature variability and environmental trends.

### California Solar Suitability Analysis

Applies terrain-based modeling techniques to identify optimal solar development locations using slope, aspect, and elevation-derived suitability metrics.

### Everglades Hydroperiod Analysis

Examines long-term hydrologic changes across the Florida Everglades through hydroperiod trend analysis and wetland monitoring.

### Louisiana Population Exposure Analysis

Identifies population concentrations exposed to environmental hazards through the integration of population density and environmental risk factors.

### Louisiana Sea Level Rise Analysis

Models potential inundation scenarios under multiple sea-level-rise conditions to support coastal resilience and adaptation planning.

### Rocky Mountain National Park Soil Erosion Analysis

Evaluates terrain-driven erosion susceptibility using slope and vegetation indicators to identify areas vulnerable to soil degradation.
