# Datasets and Data Sources

## Purpose

This document summarizes the primary datasets utilized throughout the Climate GIS Portfolio and provides transparency regarding data origin, processing, and analytical application.

---

## Climate and Environmental Datasets

### TerraClimate

Provider:
University of Idaho

Applications:

* California Seasonal Temperature Analysis
* Climate trend evaluation
* Temperature anomaly calculations

Variables Used:

* Minimum temperature
* Maximum temperature
* Mean temperature

Resolution:

Approximately 4 km

---

### SRTM Digital Elevation Model

Provider:

NASA Shuttle Radar Topography Mission

Applications:

* Solar suitability analysis
* Sea-level-rise analysis
* Terrain modeling
* Erosion susceptibility assessment

Resolution:

30 meters

---

### Landsat Collection

Provider:

United States Geological Survey (USGS)

Applications:

* Everglades hydroperiod assessment
* Vegetation monitoring
* Environmental change detection

Resolution:

30 meters

---

### JRC Global Surface Water

Provider:

European Commission Joint Research Centre

Applications:

* Hydroperiod analysis
* Surface water persistence mapping
* Wetland monitoring

Resolution:

30 meters

---

### WorldPop Population Dataset

Provider:

WorldPop

Applications:

* Louisiana population exposure analysis
* Vulnerability assessment
* Population hotspot mapping

Resolution:

100 meters

---

### TIGER/Line Administrative Boundaries

Provider:

United States Census Bureau

Applications:

* County summaries
* Parish summaries
* Regional statistics

---

### Protected Areas Data

Provider:

National Park Service

Applications:

* Rocky Mountain National Park study area
* Environmental risk assessment

---
